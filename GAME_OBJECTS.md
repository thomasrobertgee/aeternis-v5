## 4. Starting Gear & Initial Quest
Every Traveller begins their journey with basic survival equipment and a primary directive.

### Starting Equipment
*   **Heavy Iron Axe** (Common Weapon): A weighted tool of survival, blunt but effective. (+12 Attack)
*   **Reflective Turnout Gear** (Common Armor): Repurposed firefighter gear. (+8 Defense)

### Initial Quest: "Secure the West"
*   **Objective**: Defeat 3 enemies in the **Rust Fields**.
*   **Rewards**: 150 XP, 1500 Aetium (Gold).

## 5. Skills (Manifestations)
*Skills are currently being integrated into the gameplay loop. The following structure is defined:*

*   **Type**: Passive (Always on) or Active (Requires Imaginum).
*   **Rank**: Skills scale in power as they are used/leveled.
*   **manifestation**: Skills "shimmer" into existence through the player's connection to the HUD.
